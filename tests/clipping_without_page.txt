How Not to Die : Discover the Foods Scientifically Proven to Prevent and Reverse Disease  (Greger, Michael; Stone, Gene)
- Your Highlight on Location 134-138 | Added on Thursday, September 15, 2016 11:42:45 PM

Back in 1903, Thomas Edison predicted that the “doctor of the future will give no medicine, but will instruct his patient in the care of [the] human frame in diet and in the cause and prevention of diseases.”12 Sadly, all it takes is a few minutes watching pharmaceutical ads on television imploring viewers to “ask your doctor” about this or that drug to know that Edison’s prediction hasn’t come true.
